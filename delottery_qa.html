DeLottery Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is the winning prize of DeLottery ?
    The prize is DL token and there are two types of winning prize in DeLottery:
    1. Jackpot prize: jackpot prize is 70% of the total lottery pool, namely 70% of the token contact's DL balance, only one jackpot winner is drawn every round.
    2. Luckydog prize: 10% of the total lottery tickets are drawn as luckydog winners, and 9% of the lottery pool is divided equally for them as luckydog prize.
2. What’s the winning probability ?
    The jackpot winning probability is: 1 / count of tickets * 1.3
    There is a 30% probability that the drawing may fail and the lottery pool will continue increasing to the next round, which will make next jackpot more attractive. If the pool is over 10% of the token’s total supply, the winning probability will be 1 / count of tickets to avoid an unlimited prize.
    The luckydog winning probability is 10%.
3. Where does the lottery pool come from ?
    The lottery pool is filled by taxes on transactions of DL token and 20% of the pool always remains to the next round as initial pool.
    Tax Rates：
      transfer tax: 5%
      buy tax: 5%
      sell tax: 8%
    Note: to prevent whales, every transaction or address balance beyond 2% of the total supply are taxed with double rate, and 5% with fourfold rate.
4. How to buy a Delottery ticket ?
    There are two ways to buy a DeLottery ticket:
    1. Transfer ticket amount of DeLottery token (DL) to the token's contract address.
    2. Buy DL on Pancakeswap, and if the tax (5% of the trading amount) is equal or greater than the current ticket amount, the buyer address gets a lottery ticket.

    The ticket amount is a property of the contract, which is recalculated on every lottery drawing to fix it to a certain amount USD of DL tokens, you can get the current ticket amount on the official website https://de-lottery.eth.limo or in the DeLottery telegram channel https://t.me/DeLottery, you can also directly read it from the blockchain.
5. Can I buy more than one ticket with a wallet address ?
    Yes, you can buy as many tickets as you want with an address.
6. What is the buy or sell slippage ?
    12 for buy and sell to avoid price fluctuation.
7. Why can’t I sell my DL token ?
    Try to specify the amount of DL token instead of the amount of out token, and for jackpot winners, there is a 24-hour liner lock to transfer or sell the prize DL tokens so as to delay the sell pressure.
8. Which token should I buy with ?
    The liquidity pair is DL-USDC, so it’s recommended to buy with USDC, but you can also buy with other tokens.
9. If the drawing skips to the next round, is my ticket still valid for it ?
    Yes, your ticket is valid until a successful drawing.
10. How to check whether I have a ticket or my ticket numbers ?
    You can check your address's ticket numbers on the official website https://de-lottery.eth.limo or by sending your address to DeLottery telegram group https://t.me/DeLotteryGroup, you can also directly call checkAddressTicket function to the contract with web3.
11. Do I need to hold a minimum amount of DL to win the prize ?
    No, you can win the prize if you have a ticket even you're holding no DL.
12. When is the drawing and how to know if I win a prize ?
    DeLottery is automatically drawn at about 14:00 UTC everyday, and the result will be recorded in the contract's properties. You can check the winners on the official website https://de-lottery.eth.limo or in DeLottery telegram channel https://t.me/DeLottery, you can also directly read it from the blockchain.
13. How does DeLottery ensure randomness ?
    DeLottery depends on the Chainlink VRF (Verifiable Random Function) to ensure randomness. Read more details about it in the whitepaper.
