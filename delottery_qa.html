DeLottery Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is the winning prize of DeLottery ?
    The prize is DL token and there are two types of winning prize in DeLottery:
    1. Jackpot prize: jackpot prize is 70% of the total lottery pool, namely 70% of the token contact's DL balance, only one jackpot winner is drawn every lottery round.
    2. Luckydog prize: 10% of the total lottery tickets are drawn as luckydog winners, and 9% of the lottery pool is divided equally for them as luckydog prize.
2. What’s the winning probability ?
    The jackpot winning probability is: 1 / count of tickets * 1.3
    There is a 30% probability that the drawing may fail and the lottery pool will continue increasing to the next round, which will make next jackpot more attractive. If the pool is over 10% of the token’s total supply, the winning probability will be 1 / count of tickets to avoid unlimited prize.
    The luckydog winning probability is 10%.
3. Where does the lottery pool come from ?
    The lottery pool is filled by taxes on transactions of DL token and 20% of the pool always remains to the next round as initial pool.
    Tax Rates：
      transfer tax: 5%
      buy tax: 5%
      sell tax: 8%
    Note: to prevent whales, every transaction or address balance beyond 2% of the total supply are taxed with double rate, and 5% with fourfold rate.
4. How to buy a lottery ticket ?
    There are two ways to buy a DeLottery ticket:
    1. Transfer the ticket amount of DeLottery token (DL) to the token's contract address.
    2. Buy DL on Pancakeswap, and if the tax amount (5% of trading amount) is equal or greater than the current ticket amount, the buyer address gets a lottery ticket.

    The ticket amount is a property of the contract, which is recalculated on every lottery drawing to fix it to a certain amount USD of DL tokens, you can get the current ticket amount on the official website https://de-lottery.eth.link or in the DeLottery telegram channel https://t.me/DeLottery, you can also directly read it from the blockchain.
5. Can I buy more than one ticket with a wallet address ?
    Yes, you can buy as many tickets as you want with an address.
6. What is the buy or sell slippage ?
    12 for buy and sell to avoid price fluctuation.
7. Which token should I buy with ?
    The liquidity pair is DL-USDC so it’s recommended to buy with USDC, but you can also buy with other tokens.
8. If the drawing skips to next round, is my ticket still valid for it ?
    Yes, your ticket is valid until a successful drawing.
9. How to check whether I have a ticket or my ticket numbers ?
    You can check your address's ticket numbers on the official website https://de-lottery.eth.link or by sending your address to DeLottery telegram group https://t.me/DeLotteryGroup or calling checkAddressTicket function directly to the contract with web3.
10. Do I need to hold a minimum amount of DL to win the prize ?
    No, you can win the prize if you have a ticket even you're holding no DL.
11. When is the drawing and how to know if I win a prize ?
    DeLottery (DL) is automatically drawn at 14:00 UTC everyday. The drawing result will be recorded in the contract's properties, which you can read from the blockchain, and posted on the official website https://de-lottery.eth.link and in DeLottery telegram channel https://t.me/DeLottery, where you can check the winners.
12. How does DeLottery ensure randomness ?
    DeLottery depends on the Chainlink VRF (Verifiable Random Function) to ensure randomness.
13. Why can’t I sell prize DL tokens ?
    There is a 24-hour liner lock for the jackpot winner to transfer or sell the prize DL tokens to delay the sell pressure.
